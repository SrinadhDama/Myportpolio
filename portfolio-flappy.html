  • Project Title: Flappy Bird
  • Project date: 07 June, 2023

Project Information

"Flappy Game" is a simple, addictive side-scrolling game where players control a bird, guiding it through gaps between pipes by tapping the screen to keep the bird in flight. The objective is to avoid crashing into the pipes or the ground, aiming for the highest score possible. The game's minimalistic design and challenging gameplay make it engaging and fun. It's an excellent project for learning game development fundamentals.
